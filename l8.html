Tesla Model S Guide

Overview

The Tesla Model S is a fully electric luxury sedan that redefines performance, technology, and sustainability. Known for its blistering acceleration, long-range capabilities, and cutting-edge features, the Model S is a pioneer in the electric vehicle market.

Key Features

  • Electric Performance: Instant torque and acceleration, with 0-60 mph in as little as 1.99 seconds (Plaid variant).
  • Long Range: Up to 405 miles of range on a single charge (Long Range variant).
  • Autopilot: Advanced driver-assistance features, including adaptive cruise control and lane-keeping assist.
  • Interior: Minimalist design with a 17-inch touchscreen, premium materials, and panoramic glass roof.
  • Charging: Access to Tesla's Supercharger network for fast and convenient charging.

Performance

  • Acceleration: 0-60 mph in 1.99 seconds (Plaid variant).
  • Top Speed: Up to 200 mph (Plaid variant).
  • Range: Up to 405 miles (Long Range variant).
  • Powertrain: Dual or tri-motor all-wheel drive.
  • Battery: Long-lasting lithium-ion battery with advanced thermal management.

Technology

  • Infotainment: 17-inch touchscreen with gaming, streaming, and navigation capabilities.
  • Autopilot: Full self-driving capabilities (optional upgrade).
  • Connectivity: Over-the-air software updates and premium connectivity features.
  • Safety: Comprehensive safety features, including collision avoidance and emergency braking.
  • Customization: Multiple driving modes and customizable settings for a personalized experience.
