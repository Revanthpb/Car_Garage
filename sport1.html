Key Features

  • Hybrid Powertrain: Combines a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine with three electric motors, delivering a combined output of 986 horsepower.
  • All-Wheel Drive: The electric motors power the front wheels, making it Ferrari's first all-wheel-drive sports car.
  • Lightweight Design: Features advanced materials like carbon fiber and aluminum to keep weight low and performance high.
  • Electric-Only Mode: Can travel up to 15 miles (25 km) on electric power alone, making it ideal for city driving.
  • Aerodynamics: Incorporates advanced aerodynamic features, including a rear spoiler and active flaps, to optimize performance and stability.

Performance

  • 0-60 mph: 2.5 seconds.
  • Top Speed: 211 mph (340 km/h).
  • Power Output: 986 horsepower.
  • Torque: 590 lb-ft.
  • Transmission: 8-speed dual-clutch automatic.
