Maserati Quattroporte Guide

Overview

The Maserati Quattroporte is a luxury sports sedan that combines Italian craftsmanship, exhilarating performance, and cutting-edge technology. Known for its bold design and refined driving experience, the Quattroporte is a true symbol of elegance and power.

Key Features

  • Luxurious Interior: Handcrafted leather seats, premium materials, and ambient lighting create a sophisticated and comfortable cabin.
  • Advanced Technology: Features a 10.1-inch touchscreen, Harman Kardon® premium audio system, and voice-activated controls.
  • Comfort: Equipped with heated and ventilated seats, four-zone climate control, and a panoramic sunroof.
  • Performance: Offers a range of powerful engines, including a twin-turbo V8, for an exhilarating driving experience.
  • Safety: Comprehensive safety features, including adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and a 360-degree camera system.

Performance

  • Engine Options: Range includes a 3.0-liter V6 and a 3.8-liter twin-turbo V8.
  • Power Output: Up to 580 horsepower in the GTS variant.
  • Transmission: 8-speed automatic with rear-wheel or all-wheel drive.
  • Acceleration: 0-60 mph in as little as 4.2 seconds (GTS variant).
  • Top Speed: Up to 203 mph (327 km/h).

Technology

  • Infotainment System: 10.1-inch touchscreen with navigation, Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and voice commands.
  • Audio System: Harman Kardon® premium surround sound system with up to 19 speakers.
  • Driver Assistance: Includes adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and automatic emergency braking.
  • Comfort Features: Equipped with massaging seats, advanced climate control, and ambient lighting with customizable colors.
  • Safety: Comprehensive safety features, including airbags, ABS, and stability control.
