Jaguar XJ Guide

Overview

The Jaguar XJ is a luxury sedan that combines timeless elegance with cutting-edge technology. Known for its refined performance, opulent interior, and iconic design, the XJ is a symbol of sophistication and prestige.

Key Features

  • Luxurious Interior: Handcrafted leather seats, genuine wood veneers, and ambient lighting create a serene and elegant cabin.
  • Advanced Technology: Features a 12.3-inch touchscreen, Meridian™ premium audio system, and voice-activated controls.
  • Comfort: Equipped with heated and ventilated seats, four-zone climate control, and a panoramic sunroof.
  • Performance: Offers a range of powerful engines, including a supercharged V8, for a thrilling driving experience.
  • Safety: Comprehensive safety features, including adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and a 360-degree camera system.

Performance

  • Engine Options: Range includes a 3.0-liter V6 and a 5.0-liter supercharged V8.
  • Power Output: Up to 575 horsepower in the XJR variant.
  • Transmission: 8-speed automatic with rear-wheel or all-wheel drive.
  • Acceleration: 0-60 mph in as little as 4.4 seconds (XJR variant).
  • Top Speed: Electronically limited to 174 mph (280 km/h).

Technology

  • Infotainment System: 12.3-inch touchscreen with navigation, Apple CarPlay, Android Auto, and voice commands.
  • Audio System: Meridian™ premium surround sound system with up to 26 speakers.
  • Driver Assistance: Includes adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and automatic emergency braking.
  • Comfort Features: Equipped with massaging seats, advanced climate control, and ambient lighting with customizable colors.
  • Safety: Comprehensive safety features, including airbags, ABS, and stability control.
